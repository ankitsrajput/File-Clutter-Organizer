Ye nayi nayi mulaqat ka asar hai kaisa
Ban gya hu shakhs abar-e-aazoor[cloudwithoutrain] ke jaisa

Hun kasoorwar main mere in halaton ka Khud 
Jo Uski Baaton me akar mohabbat kar baitha


Mujhe dikha na koi shakhs is Jahan me fir
Jab pahli dafaa uska chehra thaa dekha

jo ek mar-tawa uska deedar karta hai
Fir kbhi palat ke usne ye Jahan na dekha


Main meri khushiyon ke katil ka israar karta hu
Jab aaine me khud ka deedar Karta hu

Maut chikhati hai rah-rah ke mujhe bulane ko
Main har bar maut ko inkar karta hu


Koi poochta hai mere in halaton ka anzaam
Ye sakhs duniya ko khud ka talabgar karta hain


Yu to chahenge use lakho mujhse jyada
Par Un sabse jyada ye shakhs use pyar karta hai
